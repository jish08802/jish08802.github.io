Getting Started as a Visually Impaired Person (VIP)


It’s easy to get started as a Visually Impaired Participant or VIP as we call them.  Foresight works with legally blind and low vision individuals who are Level 4 skiers or above - that means you can put together a series of turns and stop on your own.  If you've never skied before, haven't skied in several years or haven't skied since losing your sight we recommend booking an instructor with Vail's Adaptive Ski School.  Once you have mastered the basic skills and reached a Level 4 Foresight will step in and provide guiding services at Vail or Beaver Creek Resorts.

Filling the questionnaire

For a nominal tax deductible donation, Foresight will provide lift tickets, equipment rental, transportation and discounted lodging assistance, and a trained volunteer guide and shadow for your ski trip.  Contact us by phone at 303-506-3859 or  info@foresightskiguides.org​ with your desired dates of travel and specific needs.  The next step is to complete a Participant Questionnaire and sign the required liability waivers.  We also need a letter from your doctor stating your degree of vision loss and clearing you for skiing or snowboarding.  

The questionnaire will help us
  • Learn about your visual impairment
  • Identify your skiing/snowboarding experience
  • Match you with an appropriate guide and shadow
  • Coordinate ground transportation with Epic Mountain Express (if needed)
  • Provide assistance with discounted lodging reservations​

What to do after?

Once we receive your questionnaire we will be able to confirm availability so you can book your trip.  You will then receive an itinerary with details on when and where to meet your host and your guide upon arrival in Vail or Beaver Creek.  Your host will help with everything you need for a fantastic mountain experience.

We do request all participants make a tax-deductible contribution so Foresight can continue its good work for years to come.   Our requested minimum donations are as follows:
  1. $100 for day trips
  2. $300 for up to four days
  3. $500 for 5-7 days.

What you will do during the day?

Sample Day* The following is a sample schedule of your day in program.
  • 8:00am - 8:30am - Meet Foresight host and guides at Golden Peak base area.
  • 8:30am - 9:00am - Obtain ski/snowboard equipment if necessary, review grooming report, discuss goals for the day, get ready to head out 
  • 9:00am - 9:30am Foresight guide will do an informal vision assessment and work with VIP to determine the most effective method for guiding
  • 9:30am - Ski or snowboard!
  • 12:30pm - Lunch break
  • 1:15pm - More skiing or snowboarding
  • 3:30pm - 4:00pm - Return to Golden Peak base area.  Wrap up 
 *All times are approximate and will be customized to meet the needs of the VIP.
   

Foresight Ski Guides, Inc. is a tax exempt organization as defined in Section 501(c)(3) of the US Internal Revenue Code but contact your tax adviser to be sure your specific donation is tax deductible.
